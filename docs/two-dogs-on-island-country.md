# Two Dogs on Island Country

Status: work in progress theme song

Use this as atmosphere, source imagery and possible episode structure. It is not locked script copy yet.

## Full Draft

```text
[Intro]

Mate... I reckon the world's gone a bit strange.

Strange but true, brother. Strange but true

[SFX: beer can crack, waves, dog whistle]

Caught the early boat from Cleveland
Out from Toondah cross the bay
Water sparkling like a mirror
As the light begins the day

Tourists wide-eyed with their coffees
Keen to hit the sand and roam
Two heelers hanging out the windows
Heading for their island home

Down through Goompi past the servo
Quick stop Straddie Super Sports
Every second person's either
A fisherman or making art

Kookaburras laughing loudly
From the nearby trees
Salt wind drifting through the tea trees
Bringing everybody ease

We're just two dogs on Sandy Country
With a couple cans of beer
Talking 'bout tomorrow
And why we're blooming here

Blue heeler, red heeler
Running by the sea
Chasing sticks and sunsets
Happy, wild and free

Trying to leave this old world
A bit Kai-nder than it used to be
From Me-anne-jin to Min-jerri-bah
That's what I'd like to see

[SFX: playful dogs barking]

Kids still jumping off the jetty
Someone cooking tailor fish
Sunshine dancing on the water
Every wave another wish

One old fella tells a fishing yarn
Longer every passing year
Swears he nearly caught a monster
Bout the size of two Tinnies over there

Two heelers digging giant holes
Like they're searching for lost galleon gold
Then pretending they feel guilty
When they're told to leave it alone

Tail wag rhythm round the campfire
Dusty paws beside the flame
Spirit animals with a nickname
No two are quite the same

We're just two dogs on Island Country
With a couple cans of beer
Talking 'bout the future
With the people we hold dear

Blue heeler, red heeler
Running through the years
Finding joyful abundance
Somewhere between the laughs and tears

Trying to leave this old world
Better than it used to be
From Pulan to Mooloomba and
From Goompi to the world
Dogs and allies giving life a real fair go

Sometimes the whole world feels tired
Like everybody's lost the plot
People staring into little screens
Forgetting what they've got

Old mate solving all the world's problems
Fourth beer deep down at the pub
Pointing at stars like Galileo
While spilling half his rum

But then somebody shares a feed
Or helps a mate who's down
And suddenly the world feels lighter
When community comes round

Old dogs teaching young dogs
Where the safe tracks always lead
Stick together through the hard times
That's the only rule we need

Young dogs teaching old dogs new tricks
about how the times have changed
scam alerts and gadget tips
in words we understand

[SFX: 2 dogs howl happily oww ow ooooww]

We're just two dogs on Island Country
With a couple cans of beer
Watching whales roll past the headland
This time every year

Blue heeler, red heeler
Still kicking up the sand
Trying to build a joyful future
Right here in this land

From Me-anne-jin down to Min-jerri-bah
From Min-jerri-bah to the world
Still finding blooming magic
Every time the tide unfurls

[Outro]

You reckon we solved anything?

Nah... Probably not...

Reckon we had a decent yarn though.

Too right mate.

Too right indeed.

[SFX: kookaburra laugh, waves, distant whistle]
```
