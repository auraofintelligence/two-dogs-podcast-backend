This is the front door for the Two Dogs Podcast planning work. The episode pages are first-draft discussion surfaces for Luke and Angel; the builders are the forms that create and update the Markdown files behind them.

Discussion drafts: first-pass pages for Luke and Angel to talk through, not complete episode plans.

Regular show bits: standing bits like News Flash, Good Dog, Bad Dog, weather, sport, music and Dogs And Allies.

Micro scenes: tiny cutaways like postie arrivals, research jumps, gate rattles, walks, sticks, treats and quick dog logic.

Recording cockpit: live timer, runsheet sync, scene-cut marker, session log and programmable touch buttons for recording day.

Builders: interactive forms for episode, scene, micro scene, ad/sponsor, segment, source, guest and handoff Markdown.

Film Club: the Strange But True sample-world doorway that links back into this Two Dogs workbench.
