## Show Frame

The show is hosted by Blue Dog and Red Dog: two curious dogs attempting to understand existence, civilisation, culture, the universe and the strange behaviour of living things.

Humans are a major area of interest because they keep reshaping the planet through technology, institutions, stories, inventions and collective behaviour. They are also only one part of the picture.

Guests join the world of the show by choosing their own spirit animal or creature character. That animal is part of how they see the world, not something the system assigns to them.

The planning pages are invitations and prompts. Nothing here speaks for Luke or Angel; it just gives them things to pick up, reject, reshape or riff from.

## Dog Headlines

The weekly dog headline board is generated by:

```powershell
python tools/refresh-dog-headlines.py
```

It writes:

- `assets/data/dog-headlines.json`
- `assets/data/dog-headline-archive-index.json`
- `dog-news.html`
- `dog-news-archive.html`
- `archive/YYYY-MM-DD-dog-headline-sniff.md`

GitHub Actions runs the same refresh once per week, updates the visible page, and archives the run as Markdown.

## Folders

- [builders](./builders/) - local interactive builder forms that export `.md` files.
- [runsheets](./runsheets/) - generated showrunner runsheets for structured or freestyle episodes.
- [recording-tools](./recording-tools/index.html) - live touch panel for timers, scene-cut markers, synced runsheets, session notes and programmable buttons.
- [scenes](./scenes/) - generated animated scene ideas, cold opens, transitions, micro-scenes and visual moments.
- [guests](./guests/) - generated guest notes and guest-chosen spirit animal details.
- [ads-sponsors](./ads-sponsors/) - generated ad reads, sponsor ideas and ethical fit notes.
- [segments](./segments/) - generated recurring segment ideas, famous dog references and reusable show bits.
- [source-references](./source-references/) - generated source trails, navigation routes and deeper dataset pointers.
- [agents](./agents/) - simple agent roles and handoff prompts.
- [handoffs](./handoffs/) - short task packets passed between agents or future sessions.
- [docs](./docs/) - premise, boundaries, voice notes and theme material.
- [archive](./archive/) - weekly dog headline sniff archives.
