Episode Seed

Why this episode

The Strange But True AI guide plan is a good podcast topic because it sits right on the line between technology, trust and plain-language service.

It also lets Red Dog talk about responsible AI without making the episode feel like a lecture.

Red Dog angle

Red Dog can describe what an AI guide should and should not do: explain pages, shape enquiries, suggest service paths and protect private information.

Main beats

  • Why "clearly labelled as AI" matters.
  • How a guide can be friendly without pretending to be Luke.
  • What should be handled by a form, a meeting or a real person.
  • Why boundaries make an AI system more useful, not less useful.

Next action

Turn this into a simple recurring segment about "what the AI should hand off to humans".
