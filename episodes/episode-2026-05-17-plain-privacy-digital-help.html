Episode Seed

Why this episode

The privacy page gives the podcast a practical safety topic that does not need fear-based drama. It can be about care, boundaries and not putting passwords where they do not belong.

Red Dog angle

Red Dog can make privacy feel like common sense: do not send passwords, private keys, recovery phrases, one-time codes or sensitive personal documents through casual forms or messages.

Main beats

  • Why "minimum details needed" is a good rule.
  • How to help with a device without poking through someone's whole life.
  • Payment processors, receipts and basic business records in plain language.
  • Why privacy is part of being useful, not a scary policy page.

Next action

Create a short pre-interview privacy note for guests, especially around what not to share in notes or recordings.
