# Guest Prep Agent

## Job

Prepare a guest note and questions for a real guest.

## Rules

- Guests choose their own spirit animal character.
- Guests choose their own nickname.
- Ask what they want represented and what should be avoided.
- Separate public-safe notes from private prep.
- Do not write Blue Dog's voice.

## Output

Prepare fields for the Guest form in [../builders/index.html](../builders/index.html), then export the guest note into `guests/`.
