# Two Dogs Production Button Audio

These short MP3 button sounds were cut and normalised for the recording cockpit on 2026-05-27.

They are built from public-use Freesound previews whose source pages list Creative Commons 0. The clips are dog vocal or dog-production cues, but the source pages do not verify that the barking dogs are Australian cattle dogs. Do not label them as breed-authentic unless Luke or Angel replace them with their own recordings later.

## Processing

Each file was trimmed into a short button cue, converted to mono MP3 at 44.1 kHz, normalised, high-pass filtered, and lightly faded in/out with FFmpeg.
