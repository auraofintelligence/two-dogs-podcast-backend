Standing show rhythm

Recurring scenes for news, jokes, wins, warnings and useful little doorways.

Use these as repeatable Two Dogs scene lanes. They are not locked scripts. Red Dog can prep the doorway and source check; Blue Dog stays open for Angel to direct.

Current material needs a fresh check. News, weather, sports, public claims and Bad Dog items should be verified on recording day. Use official or credible sources, and keep developing stories clearly labelled.

Scene menu

Pick the bit that gives the episode rhythm.

CurrentNews Flash

What happened, why it matters, and what a normal person should watch next.

ComedyComedy Minute

One quick joke, visual gag or absurd plain-English translation before the dense idea returns.

AccountabilityBad Dog

Corruption, bad choices, avoidable accidents, risky moves and poor process, handled with careful sourcing.

WinsGood Dog

Good news, achievements, community care, useful inventions and people giving life a fair go.

CalendarUN World Day Of Whatever

An official observance becomes a doorway into the episode, once checked against the UN list.

SportSports Desk

Scores, clubs, rivalries, community health and what the game teaches about people.

IslandWeather Window

Storms, heat, tides, ferry mood, outdoor screenings and humility from the sky.

SoundMusic Drop

Theme-song cues, local tracks, release memory, guest songs and soundtrack beds.

ScreenFilm Club

Films, shorts, animation tests, outdoor cinema and the question: what would this look like as a scene?

VisualArt Show

Exhibitions, posters, murals, props and visual ideas that make the topic easier to feel.

PlayGames Table

Tabletop ideas, public ledger games, simulation toys and playful decision tests.

EvidenceScience Sniff Test

What is the claim, what is the evidence, and what are we not allowed to say yet?

UsefulLife Hack

One practical tip in words we understand: scams, gadgets, forms, repairs or everyday AI use.

Front doorOnboarding

How the show works, how guests choose animals, and how people can join without feeling silly.

SupportMerch Table

Shirts, stickers, posters, digital support bundles and joke products that should stay grounded.

CommunityDogs And Allies

Shout-outs, helpers, guests, builders, clubs and allies, with consent before naming people.

Simple run sheet

Use the scenes like a rhythm section.

Open

Start with News Flash, Weather Window, Good Dog or Bad Dog if the day has a clear doorway.

Bridge

Drop Comedy Minute, Life Hack, Music Drop or Science Sniff Test when the topic gets too dense.

Close

Finish with Good Dog, Dogs And Allies, Onboarding or a simple next useful action.
